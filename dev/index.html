AtmosphericDynamics.jl

Models of atmospheric dynamics implemented in ModelingToolkit.jl, part of the EarthSciML ecosystem.

Components

  • Atmospheric Fundamentals: Ideal gas law, scale height, barometric formula, mixing ratios, saturation vapor pressure, and relative humidity (Seinfeld & Pandis Ch. 1)
  • General Circulation of the Atmosphere: Coriolis parameter, geostrophic wind, and thermal wind relations (Seinfeld & Pandis Ch. 21)
  • Local Scale Meteorology: Atmospheric stability, Monin-Obukhov similarity theory, and Pasquill stability classes (Seinfeld & Pandis Ch. 16)
  • Atmospheric Diffusion Parameterizations: Wind profiles and eddy diffusivities for unstable, neutral, and stable conditions (Seinfeld & Pandis Ch. 18)
  • Global Cycles: Sulfur and Carbon: Sulfur cycle, carbon cycle, and four-compartment atmosphere models (Seinfeld & Pandis Ch. 22)
  • Holtslag & Boville (1993) Boundary Layer Diffusion: Surface fluxes, local diffusion, and nonlocal boundary layer schemes
